Task – Disease (COVID) Prediction
●   Given survey results in the past 3 days in a speciﬁc state in U.S., then
    predict the percentage of new tested positive cases in the 3 rd day.
●   Source: Delphi group @ CMU
●   Do NOT search for or use additional data for training or the answers for
    the testing data.
●   The LLM agent serves as your representative. If it violates the rules, it's as
    if you did.
                                                                                 One-hot Encoding
Dataset - Features (1)                                                                AL = [ 1 0 0 ]
                                                                                      AZ = [ 0 1 0 ]
❏   States (35, encoded to one-hot vectors)                                           CA = [ 0 0 1 ]
❏   Health Symptoms
    cli – “COVID-like illness” indicator (e.g., fever, cough, shortness of breath).
    ili – “Influenza-like illness” indicator (e.g., fever plus cough/sore throat).

❏   Community Illness Exposure
    wnohh_cmnty_cli – Weighted proportion of non-household community members known to have COVID-like
    illness.
    hh_cmnty_cli – Whether someone in the household has COVID-like illness.
    nohh_cmnty_cli – Whether someone outside the household (in the community) has COVID-like illness.
Dataset - Features (2)
❏   Beliefs and Perceptions
    wbelief_masking_effective – Weighted belief that mask-wearing is effective at preventing COVID spread.
    wbelief_distancing_effective – Weighted belief that social distancing is effective.
    wcovid_vaccinated_friends – Weighted estimate of proportion of friends vaccinated against COVID.
    wworried_catch_covid – Level of worry about catching COVID.
    worried_finances – Level of worry about personal or household finances.
    wothers_masked_public – Perception of how many other people in public wore masks.
    wothers_distanced_public – Perception of how many other people maintained physical distance in public.

❏ Behaviors and Activities
    wlarge_event_indoors – Whether the respondent attended a large indoor event.
    wshop_indoors – Frequency or indicator of shopping indoors (e.g., grocery store).
    wrestaurant_indoors – Frequency or indicator of eating at an indoor restaurant.
    wearing_mask_7d – Whether respondent wore a mask in the past 7 days.
    public_transit – Whether respondent used public transportation.

❏ Tested Positive Cases
    tested_positive_dayX – Whether the respondent tested positive for COVID-19 at that day.
